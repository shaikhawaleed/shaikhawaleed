How I thought of Fashion as a career?

When the pandameic started, I enrolled in a couple of virtual courses on fashion, materials, and trends. This was the point at which I considered starting my own business.

I started studying fabrics and materials in greater depth. I did a lot of research and visited a lot of stores in Bahrain to see what the differences were, what was better, and what the prices were. It quickly became my primary passion, and whenever I had free time, I would either research and read about it or visit stores to view the materials in person.
