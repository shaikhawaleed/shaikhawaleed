My Journey

I began my career in fashion a year ago, when Covid quarantined us and left us with nothing to do, I decided to take advantage of the situation and start my own small business I began creating my own designs, purchasing fabric, and looking for the ideal tailor.

I didn't think I'd succeed at first, since my style isn't typical; I prefer to stand out. I also didn't think others would enjoy the designs because our community has a distinct style and rarely deviates from it.

Surprisingly, people actually loved my designs, and I attracted customers, despite the fact that my sales weren't always high, but I felt it was great at first.

My First Piece

This was the first piece i designed & sold. I didn’t think people from Bahrain would buy it as its a bright color and they would prefer something darker. But it actually was my best seller dress for 2 months straight. In the first picture you could see the photo quality wasn’t clear and professional, the background isn’t really suitable for a business. And you can see the dress was a bit wrinkled.

As a beginner i didn’t think these little details would matter, I thought all that mattered was the quality of the dress, fabric and design, but it turns out that these little details were all that mattered. I went through alot ups & downs. Many dresses I designed didn’t come as expected. I didn’t know whats the perfect profit for me, as my main focus was on the product itself.
