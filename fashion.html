Fashion And Designing Basics

In this page, I'll discuss my interest for fashion and design, as well as my experience with it.

Fashion Design:
Fashion Design is the art of applying your unique touches while designing and ceating your peice.

Because I was so passionate towards fashion, I dug even deeper and looked into the various types of fashion industries. I was well-versed in the creation, promotion, and production of styles. This, in my opinion, is the most important research that everyone interested in fashion should conduct.

The four main elements in fashion are:


Each one of these elements represents a huge part in fashion.

Texture:
Texture is an important element as it enhances the beauty of the design.
Design:
Design is the main purpose of fashion as it influences the society.
Style:
Your style reflects your self, as said by many "Your outfit is self-expression".
Color:
Color is the most important element as it attracts and leads the customer towards the product.
